My Project So Far

+
-

I developed a reverse vending machine integrated with the Recycle Creations app to tackle plastic and can waste. This innovative system encourages recycling by providing users with monetary rewards for their contributions.

The machine is designed to efficiently sort and process recyclable materials while seamlessly connecting to the Recycle Creations app. Through this integration, users can track their recycling activity, earn incentives, and contribute to a more sustainable environment.

By leveraging IoT technology, the system ensures accurate data collection and real-time updates, making recycling more accessible and rewarding. This project reflects my commitment to utilizing technology for environmental sustainability and fostering a circular economy.

In addition to the RVM, I have developed SISUKMA, an internal document management system for ITK, and a correspondence system for Bankaltimtara to streamline official communications.

I also created CinemaDhika, a web-based platform for movie listings, a personal portfolio website, and an iPhone promotional website to showcase marketing strategies through web development.

Furthermore, I built Pesat, an application for tracking student tardiness, and Nitip, a mobile app that facilitates personal shopping services, highlighting my ability to develop practical and user-friendly digital solutions.

These projects demonstrate my expertise in IoT, web, and mobile application development, as well as my dedication to creating technology-driven solutions that provide real-world impact.
